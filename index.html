About Me

I am Joydeep Sen Gupta, an undergrad student currently residing in New Delhi, India. I am an open-source advocate and contributor to various FOSS projects. My primary interests include web development, front-end development, Unix/Linux administration & cybersecurity.

I LOVE computers and have, since my childhood, been fascinated by them. To me writing a few lines of code and watch the computer DO what I want to do is purely a magical experience.

I enjoy spending time pondering over tough problems, especially in the field of programming. I also believe in the Unix philosophy of doing things i.e. write programs that do one thing and do it well.

Projects

  • vauquelin a CSS themed material design theme for GNOME and GTK-based desktop environments.

  • jbdwm a custom build of the dynamic window manager, written in C. I am actively maintaining this project.

  • dac-software a data acquisition and control software using LabVIEW to measure temperature and pressure from a thermocouple and pressure transmitter, respectively.

  • backup-server-setup a feasible and efficient incremental backup solution for backing up any kind of data.
